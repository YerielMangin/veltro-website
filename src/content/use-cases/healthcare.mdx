# Reliability where Life Depends on It

Healthcare facilities operate under intense scrutiny and zero-tolerance for failure. Veltro provides the clinical precision required to manage life-critical assets and maintain strict regulatory compliance.

## Industry Challenges

- **Critical asset uptime** (Imaging, HVAC, Power)
- **Strict Joint Commission/Regulatory compliance**
- **Infection control** protocols for maintenance work
- **Multi-layered reporting** for different stakeholders

## The Veltro Solution

### Audit-Ready, Always
Veltro maintains a comprehensive, immutable audit trail for every asset. Generate compliance reports for inspectors in a single click.

### Specialized Protocols
Define infection control and safety protocols for specific zones. Technicians receive clinical requirements directly on their mobile work orders before starting work.

### High-Priority Dispatching
Rhythm means knowing what's urgent. Veltro's intelligent dispatching ensures critical medical gas or power issues are prioritized above routine facility maintenance.
