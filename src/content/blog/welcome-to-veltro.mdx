# Why We Built Veltro

Maintenance management hasn't changed in decades. Most teams still rely on spreadsheets, whiteboards, or legacy software built in the 90s.

We built Veltro to change that.

## The Problem

- **Spreadsheets** can't track work orders across a team
- **Legacy CMMS** software is slow, ugly, and expensive
- **Generic project tools** weren't built for maintenance workflows

## Our Approach

Veltro is built from the ground up as a modern, multi-tenant SaaS platform:

- **Real-time** — see work order status updates instantly
- **Mobile-first** — field techs can update from their phone
- **Secure** — row-level tenant isolation, audit logs, SOC 2 compliance
- **Affordable** — starts at $24/user/month
